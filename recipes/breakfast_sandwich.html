Maple French Toast Sandwich

Description

A sandwich with all the staples of breakfast.

Ingredients List

  • 8 slices of maple-cured bacon
  • 7 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon ground nutmeg
  • 4 slices of bread
  • 5 tablespoons of oil, or as needed
  • 1 tablespoon of maple syrup, or to taste
